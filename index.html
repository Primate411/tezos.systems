What is tz4 / BLS Baking?

tz4 accounts use BLS signatures, which have a unique property: they can be aggregated. This means multiple signatures can be combined into one.

Why it matters:

  • 63x less bandwidth: From 900 MB/day to just 14 MB/day for bakers
  • Single signature: Replaces ~200 attestation signatures per block with one
  • More secure: Enables all bakers to attest every block (with Tallinn upgrade)
  • Leaner consensus: Faster validation and reduced storage needs

Introduced in the Seoul protocol (September 2025), tz4/BLS baking is a major step toward making Tezos more efficient and scalable.
